Steam and PC

Steam is an online video game store that allows PC users to buy popular video game titles. Steam is available for Windows, MacBook, Linux, TV, and mobile devices too. You only need an internet connection and a modern laptop to play games that take up a lot of bandwidth.

Popular Video Games on Steam:

  • Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
  • Destiny 2
  • Resident Evil 4
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II
  • Apex Legends

Nintendo

Nintendo is a video game company in Kyoto, Japan that develops and releases both video games and video game consoles. Nintendo has released many consoles over the years, including the NES, SNES, Nintendo 64, GameCube, Wii and Wii U, and the Nintendo Switch. Nintendo has many popular and recognizable characters like Mario and Luigi.

Popular Nintendo Games:

  • Mario Party Superstars
  • Ori and the Will of the Wisps
  • LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga
  • Metroid Prime Remastered
  • Minecraft

Xbox

Xbox is a video gaming brand created and owned by Microsoft. The brand consists of five video game consoles and various game titles. The brand was first introduced in the US in November 2001, with the launch of the original Xbox console.

Popular Xbox Games:

  • Fortnite
  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II
  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege
  • Apex Legends
  • ROBLOX

Playstation

Playstation is a video gaming brand that consists of five video game consoles and various, exclusive video game titles. Playstation, owned by Sony Interactive Entertainment, released the first Playstation in Japan in December 1994.

Popular Playstation Games:

  • Red Dead Redemption 1
  • Grand Theft Auto V
  • Persona 5 Royal
  • The Last of Us Remastered
  • God of War
